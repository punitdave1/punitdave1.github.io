About Me

Business Analyst with a background in finance, hands-on experience in SQL, Power BI, and real-world style BA projects including BRD/FRD, user stories and process improvement.

Who I am

I enjoy understanding problems deeply, working with data to find patterns, and translating business requirements into clear, actionable solutions for technical and business teams.

  • MBA (Finance) with an analytical mindset
  • Hands-on portfolio across E-Commerce & FinTech datasets
  • Comfortable with both documentation and data work
How I work

I start by clarifying the problem, explore data using SQL/Excel, then design dashboards, flows and documentation (BRD/FRD, user stories). I validate everything against source data and always aim for clarity.

  • Ask precise questions, avoid assumptions
  • Use SQL to validate dashboard metrics
  • Document just enough – clear, simple, maintainable
